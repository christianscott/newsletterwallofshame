The Newsletter Wall of Shame

There's one golden rule of mailing lists, and luckily it's very simple. If I click the unsubscribe link at the bottom of your email, I should never hear from you again.

Far too many companies screw this up. Here are some common near-misses that make my eyes twitch:

I have to sign in to your site before you'll unsubscribe me. You already know who I am — you're tracking every other link in the email.

You promise to stop emailing me some amount of time after I unsubscribe. Are you snail-mailing my email address to MailChimp on a hand written postcard?

You only unsubscribe me from 1 of the 10,000 mailing lists you've put me on. Believe it or not, I hate your monthly updates as much as I hate your weekly updates.

To encourage you to change your ways, here's a naughty list that you'll appear on if you violate this oh-so simple rule.
